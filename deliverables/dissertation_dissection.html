The Strategy

Why dissect the dissertation this way? Four deliberate reasons.

⚡

Speed

The data spans 2019–2021. Traditional journal submissions could take 1–2 more years. This approach gets findings into the literature rapidly — preprints establish priority, brief reports accelerate peer review.

➦

Interconnection

Each output builds on the others. The preprint is cited by both empirical papers. Study 2 builds on Study 1. The book provides historical context for everything. Together, they form a coherent body of work.

☯

Breadth of Impact

Software reaches computational researchers. Papers reach health scientists and policy makers. The book reaches the public. One dissertation, three distinct audiences.

◼

The Unified Work Remains

The dissertation itself remains the unified scholarly contribution — the single document where all three lenses converge. These outputs extend its reach without diminishing its wholeness.
